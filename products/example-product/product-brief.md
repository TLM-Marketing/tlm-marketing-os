# Product Brief: Flowdeck Signals

## Product Overview
Flowdeck Signals is an intent data layer that identifies which accounts are actively researching topics related to your product. It feeds directly into Flowdeck Core workflows, so your team can act on buying signals in real time — not days later.

## Launch Context
Signals was previously bundled into Growth and Enterprise plans only. We're now offering it as a standalone product at $499/month to capture teams that aren't ready for the full platform but want intent data connected to their existing workflows.

## Key Features for This Launch
- Website visitor identification (company-level, not individual)
- Topic-based intent signals from third-party data providers
- Account surge alerts (Slack, email, or webhook)
- Direct integration with Flowdeck Core routing rules
- New: Standalone mode with CRM push (Salesforce, HubSpot) — no Flowdeck Core required

## Target Audience for This Launch
- Primary: "David" (Marketing Ops / RevOps Lead) — needs data flowing into existing systems
- Secondary: "Amit" (Head of Sales / SDR Manager) — wants to know which accounts are hot

## Competitive Context
- 6sense and Bombora own the enterprise intent data market
- ZoomInfo has intent but it's part of a massive (and expensive) platform
- Our angle: Signals is affordable, fast to set up, and connects directly to workflows — not just a dashboard you check once a week

## Key Messages for This Launch
1. "Intent data is useless in a dashboard. Signals puts it in your workflow."
2. "Know which accounts are ready to buy — and route them to the right rep instantly."
3. "Enterprise-grade intent data at a mid-market price. $499/month, live in a week."

## Pricing
- Standalone: $499/month
- Included in Growth ($799/month) and Enterprise plans

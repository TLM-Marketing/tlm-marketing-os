# Company & Product Knowledge

## Company

**Name:** TLM.Marketing (TLM)

**One-liner:** A revenue marketing partner / AI-native GTM agency for tech companies that sell to mid-market and enterprise buyers.

**Tagline:** Turning Demand Into Revenue — AI-Native, Revenue-First Marketing for Tech Brands.

**Mission / value proposition:** TLM turns demand into revenue. Its proprietary **Demand Activation™** framework blends AI-native inbound, outbound, and analytics to turn buyer intent into pipeline — fast. From day one, TLM operates as an extension of the client's team, bringing the tools, talent, and execution needed to drive full-funnel growth.

**Business model:** Agency / outsourced revenue marketing. TLM sells GTM services to client tech companies (it is not selling a software product).

**Proof point:** $70M+ in qualified pipeline generated for clients.

**Why TLM:**
- **Proven results** — Demand Activation framework has delivered $70M+ in qualified opportunities for leading tech companies.
- **True partnership** — Operates as an extension of the client team; proactive collaboration, shared KPIs, long-term alignment, not a vendor relationship.
- **Full-funnel awareness** — Connects brand, demand, and sales activation into one seamless journey, first touch to closed won.
- **Innovative approach** — Deep AI integration and constant experimentation; rewrites playbooks instead of following them.
- **Quick ramp-up** — Impact of a full in-house team at a fraction of the cost; model covers strategy, execution, AI automation, and the cost of key third-party tools used in campaigns.

**Notable clients:** Microsoft, Cisco, AWS, Kaltura, Datricks, Logz.io, D-ID, Singular, BLEND, MazeBolt, SQream, Seraphic, Lumenis, ConsumerAffairs, and others.

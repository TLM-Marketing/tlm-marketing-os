# Target Audiences

## Primary ICP: Tech companies selling to mid-market & enterprise
TLM's clients are B2B tech companies (software, security, data, AI, infrastructure) that sell into mid-market and enterprise buyers and need a revenue marketing engine they don't want to build alone.

- **Type:** B2B tech / SaaS, security, data, AI, deep tech
- **GTM motion:** ABM + inbound + outbound into mid-market and enterprise accounts
- **Situation:** Have ambitious pipeline targets but lack the in-house team, AI tooling, or bandwidth to hit them; tired of vendors who don't own outcomes
- **What they want:** Pipeline and revenue — fast — from a partner that operates as an extension of their team
- **Reference clients:** Microsoft, Cisco, AWS, Kaltura, Datricks, Logz.io, D-ID, Singular, BLEND, MazeBolt, SQream, Seraphic

## Buyer Personas

### The CMO / VP Marketing
**Title:** CMO, VP Marketing, Head of Marketing
**Reports to:** CEO or CRO
**Goals:** Marketing-sourced pipeline, provable ROI, full-funnel growth without ballooning headcount
**Pain points:**
- Pressure to show marketing's contribution to revenue, not just MQLs
- Stretched team; too much to execute with the people they have
- Wants AI-native execution but doesn't have the talent or stack in-house
**Messaging angle:** "Turn demand into revenue — a full-funnel engine that proves its impact, run as an extension of your team."

### The CRO / VP Sales
**Title:** CRO, VP Sales, Head of Revenue
**Reports to:** CEO
**Goals:** Predictable pipeline, Sales Accepted Meetings, faster cycles
**Pain points:**
- Outbound underperforms; SDR teams are expensive and slow to ramp
- Marketing and sales aren't aligned on a single revenue KPI
- Needs meetings with accounts showing real intent, not cold lists
**Messaging angle:** "Dark-funnel intent turned into Sales Accepted Meetings — SDR-level output at half the cost."

### The Founder / CEO (esp. earlier-stage tech)
**Title:** Founder, CEO
**Reports to:** Board
**Goals:** Scalable GTM that drives revenue without a big internal build
**Pain points:**
- Can't afford or doesn't want to hire a full in-house GTM team yet
- Needs speed to pipeline and a partner who owns the outcome
- Wants AI leverage but lacks time to engineer it
**Messaging angle:** "The impact of a full in-house GTM team at a fraction of the cost — built, run, and eventually handed over to you."

### The RevOps / Demand Gen Lead
**Title:** RevOps Lead, Demand Gen Manager, Growth Marketing Manager
**Reports to:** CMO or CRO
**Goals:** Clean data, integrated workflows, campaigns that convert
**Pain points:**
- GTM stack is a patchwork; reporting is manual
- Hard to act on intent signals across channels
- Every campaign needs custom plumbing
**Messaging angle:** "AI-native inbound, outbound & analytics — fully integrated, with the data, tech, and workflows handled."

# Target Audiences

## Primary ICP

**Who:** Tech companies (B2B) that sell to mid-market and enterprise buyers.
**Situation:** Marketing is on the hook for pipeline and revenue, but the team is stretched — they need to scale GTM without adding headcount.
**Why they buy TLM:** They want an AI-native, full-funnel GTM engine (inbound + outbound + analytics) built and run as an extension of their team, with one KPI: revenue.

---

## Personas

### CMO / VP Marketing
**Goals:** Drive pipeline through AI-native GTM; prove marketing's revenue impact to the board; build a scalable GTM engine without growing headcount.
**Pain points:** Pressure to show pipeline contribution; brand vs. demand tension; rising tool/agency costs; hard to stay ahead on AI.
**What lands:** Demand Activation™ framework, $70M+ pipeline proof, "extension of your team" partnership model.

### VP / Senior Director of Revenue Marketing
**Goals:** Hit pipeline KPIs across paid inbound and outbound; tie every campaign to revenue; move faster on intent.
**Pain points:** Juggling inbound and outbound execution; needs hands-on execution plus strategic thinking; wants results, not decks.
**What lands:** Full-funnel execution under one roof, SDR-level outbound output at lower cost, analytics that prove impact.
**Voice of customer:** "Their team brings a rare blend of hands-on execution and strategic thinking that drives results." — Logz.io

### Marketing Ops / RevOps Lead
**Goals:** Operationalize intent data and GTM workflows; integrate tools, data, and dashboards; act on dark-funnel signals in real time.
**Pain points:** Disconnected tools and data; signals stuck in dashboards instead of workflows; reporting burden.
**What lands:** AI GTM Engineering (build → operate → transfer), included tooling, dashboards and workflows handed over.

### Head of Sales / SDR Leadership (influencer)
**Goals:** More Sales Accepted Meetings; faster sales cycles; only work accounts with real intent.
**Pain points:** Low-quality leads; long cycles; cost of SDR headcount.
**What lands:** Sequences triggered by real-time intent, messaging that drives replies, SDR-level output at half the cost.
